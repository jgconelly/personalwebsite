My Hobbies

Music

Music is my all time favorite activity. I started playing the recorder in 3rd grade, which then led me start playing clarinet about a year later. I developed a deep affinity with classical and romantic music. I started playing bass on my 14th birthday. I wanted to start playing all the rock and metal I kept hearing on the radio. During my time in England, I picked up Alto and Tenor sax so I could play in the big band at school. I eventually majored in clarinet and saxophone performance at Santa Clara University.

Rowing

I started rowing during my first year at Santa Clara University. It turned into an almost unhealthy obession, and the force that kept my brain together during my intense class load.

Ultimate Frisbee

Growing up around Amherst sparked my interest in Ultimate. I thought about playing during my undergrad at SCU, but instead stuck with crew. During grad school, I was able to play with the club team SCAB. I try to play pickup on Saturday and Sunday in Mountain View and Palo Alto, CA.

Reading

Reading has been a regular activity of mine since I was a kid. I am fan of science fiction/fantasy, as well as many other non-fiction books.

Currently reading: Cat's Cradle by Kurt Vonnegut, JR.
