My Hobbies

Music

Music is something that has always been a part of my life. I started playing the recorder in 3rd grade, which then led me start playing clarinet about a year later. I developed a deep afinity with classical and romantic music.

I started playing bass on my 14th birthday so I could start playing all the rock and metal I kept hearing on the radio.

During my time in England, I picked up Alto and Tenor sax so could play in the big band at school. I eventually majored in clarinet and saxophone performance at Santa Clara University.

Rowing

I started rowing during my first year at Santa Clara University. It turned into an almost unhealthy obession, and the force that kept my brain together during my intense class load.
